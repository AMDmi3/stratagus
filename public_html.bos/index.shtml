News

The Alpha prerelease of Invasion - Battle of Survival 2.0 has been released

After a long wait, a prerelease of the next version of the game is available. Get it from the download section, give it a try and tell us what you liked and the bugs you found. Note that a few things like the campaigns are not complete yet.

Invasion - Battle of Survival 2.0 integrates new features like Guichan menus, improved UI, image-based map system, new units, new set elements...
