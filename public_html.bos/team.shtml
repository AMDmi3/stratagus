The Invasion Team

Bos is an open source project open to everybody. The currently active members are:
  • François Beerten "feb", project manager
  • Tejay Penfold "TimberDragon", 3d specialist
  • Frank Loeffler "gorm", script writer and blender specialist
  • Loïs Taulelle "GruiicK", maps and campaigns creator

Tina Petersen "p0ng" started the project. She unfortunatly passed away in 2003.

Every contributor becomes in a sense a member of the project. For a detailed list of the contributions, check the svn and cvs repositories.
