The Bos Wars Team

Bos Wars is an open source project open to everybody. The currently active members are:

  • François Beerten "feb", project leader *
  • Jimmy Salmon "jim4", programmer
  • Loïs Taulelle "GruiicK", maps and campaigns creator *
  • Frank Loeffler "gorm", script writer and blender specialist
  • Nehal Mistry "Nehal", programmer
  • Tejay Penfold "TimberDragon", 3d specialist

The members with an asterisk (*) can be contacted by email. Their email address is "<nickname>@boswars.org". To contact feb, append the number one to the nickname.

Past contributors
